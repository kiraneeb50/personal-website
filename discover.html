Discover Duluth & UMD

Why I Chose UMD

I chose UMD because of the beautiful hiking trails throughout campus and around Lake Superior, as well as its unique winter atmosphere and incredible winter sports opportunities. I had previously skied and snowboarded at the local hill near Duluth, and I loved it. Since you really have to appreciate nature—and essentially the long, cold winters up here—I felt that this college would be the perfect place for me and an easy way to meet people with the same interests.

What I Like About Duluth

I love the scenery Duluth has to offer. Not only does it have downtown tourist attractions, but it also has countless parks and hiking spots throughout the area. Duluth has its own unique climate year-round, and it creates a different feeling no matter the season. Another reason I love it is the easy access to the North Shore; you can make a whole day out of exploring more of the lake, while still being within driving distance of the Twin Cities.

Personal Experiences and Highlights

Northern Lights Chasing

One of my favorite recent experiences has been going out late at night with friends to chase the northern lights. If you ever have the chance to see them, do it. It's surreal to witness such a stunning natural phenomenon in person.

Local Farmers Markets

I also love the local farmers markets. They're so fun to walk around and explore, especially with all the food vendors. Grabbing something fresh to eat while wandering around is always my favorite. There's fresh produce, bouquets of flowers, and handmade crafts or jewelry.

UMD Hockey Games

Hockey has become one of my favorite college sports to watch. I've always loved watching different sports, but D1 hockey is truly on another level. And since the team has been doing really well this season, it's been even more exciting. The student section is full of energy—especially during close games or when a fight breaks out. I'll always recommend supporting our school's teams, but the hockey games are truly a guaranteed good time.
